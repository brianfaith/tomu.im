I'm the Tomu family!

The Tomu family are a group of tiny boards designed to fit inside your USB port. All of the Tomu family has both open source firmware and open source hardware!

 

Tomu - The original Tomu

Made by Kosagi!

Fomu - The FPGA based Tomu

Made by Kosagi!

Somu - The security Tomu

Made by SoloKeys!
 

Help! Which Tomu should I get?

As there are multiple Tomu boards now, it can be confusing to figure out which board to purchase. This guide is designed to help you figure out which Tomu is right for you!

I want to build my own device

You should build your own Tomu.

I want to play with RISC-V

You should get the Fomu board.

I want to play with FPGAs

You should get the Fomu board.

I need the highest security to protect my accounts

You should purchase a Somu, as it supports the latest crypto standards and contains a real random number source.
